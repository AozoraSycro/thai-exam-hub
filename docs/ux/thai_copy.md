## Copywriting Notes & Rationale
1.  **Direct & Soft (นะ/ครับ/ค่ะ)**: Used "นะ" for a friendly, mentoring tone that resonates with high school students (Gen Z). It reduces the "exam stress" factor.
2.  **Outcome-Oriented**: Focuses on the "คณะในฝัน" (Dream Faculty) which is the ultimate emotional trigger for Thai students.
3.  **AdSense Safety**: Avoided words like "แจก" (Giveaway) or "หลุด" (Leaked) in titles, as these can sometimes trigger policy flags regarding intellectual property. Used "คลังข้อสอบ" (Exam Hub/Library) and "ฝึกทำ" (Practice) instead.
4.  **Clarity for Tutors**: Labels like "ประวัติการทำโจทย์" are clear enough for tutors to understand when reviewing a student's screen.
