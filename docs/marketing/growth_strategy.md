## 4. Referral Loop: Score-Sharing (The Viral Cyclone)
Turn every user into an acquisition channel via social proof.

### The "Challenge Your Friend" Loop
*   **Feature**: **Dynamic Score Card**.
    *   Upon finishing a quiz, generate a visual card (HTML/CSS to Image) showing:
        *   User's Score (e.g., 85/100)
        *   Percentile/Rank (e.g., "Top 5% of students today")
        *   A catchy "Status" (e.g., "The Math God" or "English Ninja")
        *   **QR Code** + URL to the exact same quiz.
*   **Trigger**: A prominent "แชร์คะแนนท้าเพื่อน" (Share Score & Challenge Friends) button on the results page.
*   **Hook**: Students love showing off high scores. If they fail, they want to see if their friends fail too.
*   **Target**: 1 share for every 5 completed quizzes (K-factor: 0.2).
