# MASTER INVENTORY LIST: THAI EDUCATIONAL DATA (ม.4-6)

## 1. Core Subjects (O-NET & A-Level)

### Mathematics
*   **Topics:** Sets (เซต), Logic (ตรรกศาสตร์), Real Number System (ระบบจำนวนจริง), Relations & Functions (ความสัมพันธ์และฟังก์ชัน), Analytic Geometry (เรขาคณิตวิเคราะห์), Conic Sections (ภาคตัดกรวย), Exponentials & Logarithms (เลขยกกำลังและลอการิทึม), Trigonometry (ตรีโกณมิติ), Matrices (เมทริกซ์), Vectors (เวกเตอร์), Complex Numbers (จำนวนเชิงซ้อน), Calculus (แคลคูลัส), Statistics & Probability (สถิติและความน่าจะเป็น).
*   **Question Patterns:** Multiple choice (ก-ง) and numeric entry. Often involves multi-step problem solving integrating 2-3 topics (e.g., Matrix + Trigonometry).

### Physics
*   **Topics:** Mechanics (Linear Motion, Newton's Laws, Gravitation, Equilibrium, Work & Energy, Momentum, Projectile, Circular, SHM), Matter (Solids, Fluids), Heat & Gas (Thermodynamics), Waves & Sound, Light (Wave & Physical Optics), Electromagnetism (Electrostatics, DC/AC Circuits, Magnetic Fields), Atomic & Nuclear Physics.
*   **Question Patterns:** Formula application, graph interpretation (v-t, a-t graphs), conceptual analysis.

### Chemistry
*   **Topics:** Atoms & Periodic Table, Chemical Bonding (Ionic, Covalent, Metallic), Stoichiometry (Moles, Formulas, Equations), States of Matter, Reaction Rates, Chemical Equilibrium, Acids-Bases, Electrochemistry, Organic Chemistry, Polymers, Solutions & Energy.
*   **Question Patterns:** Stoichiometric calculations, identifying functional groups, balancing redox equations.

### Biology
*   **Topics:** Cell Biology (Organelles, Transport, Respiration, Division), Genetics (Mendel, DNA/RNA, Protein Synthesis), Evolution & Diversity (Taxonomy, Kingdoms), Plant Biology (Structure, Transport, Photosynthesis), Animal Physiology (Digestion, Circulation, Immunity, Respiration, Excretion, Nervous, Endocrine), Ecology (Biomes, Food Webs, Population).
*   **Question Patterns:** Diagram labeling, process sequencing (e.g., Calvin cycle), experimental design analysis.

### Thai Language
*   **Topics:** Grammar (Sounds, Spelling, Word Formation, Loanwords, Sentence Structures, Royal Vocabulary), Communication (Essay, Summary, Argumentation, Reading Comprehension), Literature (Ramakien, Khun Chang Khun Phaen, Literary Devices).
*   **Question Patterns:** Reading comprehension passages, identifying correct spelling/usage, interpreting literary devices.

### Social Studies
*   **Topics:** Religion (Buddhism, Islam, Christianity, Hinduism), Civics (Constitution, Law, Human Rights, Culture), Economics (Supply/Demand, Markets, Policy, Sufficiency Economy), History (Thai History, Ancient Civilizations, World Wars), Geography (Maps, Earth Structure, Climate, Disasters).
*   **Question Patterns:** Scenario-based questions, map reading, historical cause-and-effect.

### English
*   **Topics:** Grammar (Tenses, Conditionals, Modals, Relative Clauses), Vocabulary (Academic, Idioms, Phrasal Verbs), Reading (Main Ideas, Inference, Context Clues), Writing (Error Identification), Listening/Speaking.
*   **Question Patterns:** Cloze tests, error identification, reading comprehension, conversational dialogues.

## 2. Professional Track (TPAT 1-5)

*   **TPAT 1 (Medical):** Logical & Numerical Intelligence, Medical Ethics (patient rights, scenario-based dilemmas), Reading Comprehension.
*   **TPAT 2 (Fine Arts):** Art history (Thai/Western), principles of design, color theory, visual arts analysis.
*   **TPAT 3 (Science/Engineering):** Applied physics, mechanical reasoning, spatial visualization (3D objects), logical problem solving.
*   **TPAT 4 (Architecture):** Perspective drawing, isometric views, pattern folding, architectural history.
*   **TPAT 5 (Teaching):** Teacher ethics (จรรยาบรรณวิชาชีพครู), educational psychology, classroom management scenarios.

## 5. Obscure & Specialized Topics

*   **Triam Udom Suksa Entrance Exam:** Highly advanced algebra, competitive geometry, integrates high school (ม.4) concepts into M.3 exams. Deep analytical physics.
*   **MWIT (Mahidol Wittayanusorn) Entrance Exam:** Focus on scientific methodology, experimental design, and advanced problem-solving rather than rote memorization.
